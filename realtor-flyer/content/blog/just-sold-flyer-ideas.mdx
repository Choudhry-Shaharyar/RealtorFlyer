## Strategic Distribution for Maximum Impact

### The "Ring Around the Sale" Method

This proven farming strategy works exceptionally well in established Ontario neighbourhoods:

1. **Inner Ring (Same Street)**: Hand-deliver flyers with a personal note
2. **Middle Ring (Adjacent Streets)**: Canada Post Neighbourhood Mail drop
3. **Outer Ring (Same School Catchment)**: Digital campaign targeting specific postal codes

For a typical GTA neighbourhood, this might mean:
- 20-30 hand-delivered flyers
- 200-300 mailed flyers
- 1,000+ targeted social media impressions

### Timing Your Distribution

**Day 1**: Social media announcement
**Day 2-3**: Hand-delivery to immediate neighbours
**Day 7**: Canada Post mail drop (gives time for processing)
**Day 14**: Follow-up "Market Update" email to your sphere in that area

## RECO and OREA Compliance Considerations

When creating just sold materials in Ontario, remember:

1. **Dual agency disclosure**: If you represented both sides, ensure proper disclosure
2. **Price accuracy**: Only share sold prices once the transaction has closed and data is public
3. **Client permission**: Some sellers prefer privacyâ€”always ask before featuring their home
4. **OREA forms**: Ensure your advertising complies with OREA advertising guidelines
5. **RECO registration**: Your registration number must appear on all marketing materials
